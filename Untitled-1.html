To You, As This Year Ends

As this year quietly folds itself into memory,
I find you standing in every moment that mattered.
In my chaos, you were calm.
In my silence, you were warmth.

This year wasn’t perfect—but you were my constant.
Thank you for staying, for choosing, for understanding me
even when words failed me.

If the next year brings storms, I hope we learn to dance in the rain.
If it brings calm, I hope we sit together and breathe it in.
No promises of perfection—
only this: I’m grateful for you, and I’m glad I didn’t walk this year alone.

Here’s to another chapter,
another beginning,
and to us, stepping into it together.

— Your Shadow/Foxie,always and forever
